About Us

Since 2017, we have been dedicated to discovering and supporting talented artists who push the boundaries of traditional pop rock sound. We are a team of passionate music enthusiasts who are committed to helping our artists reach their full potential and share their unique voices with the world. We believe in the power of music to bring people together and inspire change, and we strive to cultivate a diverse and inclusive community of artists and fans. We are constantly on the lookout for the next big thing in alternative and experimental Pop Rock, and we are proud to work with a talented roster of artists who are making waves in the industry. If you are an artist who is looking for a supportive and forward-thinking label to help you take your career to the next level, we encourage you to get in touch with us.
